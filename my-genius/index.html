My genius is skiing!

Skiing is a sport that is loved all over the world. There are many different types of skiing, including cross-country, backcountry, freestyle, telemark, and most famously, alpine skiing.

For the usual alpine skiing, 3 things are needed to begin your skiing journey:

  1. GEAR--skis, boots, poles, helmet, & warm clothing
  2. RESORT--learn more about the best resorts on the resources page
  3. LESSONS--beginners should take either alpine skiing lessons for downhill skiing or cross-country lessons
